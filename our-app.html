Rx MedMatch

Connect your pharmacy to a verified medication network.

Inventory sharing for verified pharmacies.

Canada wastes $1.7 billion in medications every year while patients go without. Rx MedMatch connects verified pharmacies so overstock finds a home before it expires — and pharmacies in need can source medications fast across banners, chains, and independents.

What it does
  • ✓Real-time Have/Need listings for surplus and shortage items
  • ✓Cross-banner visibility — independents and chains can connect
  • ✓Prioritizes redeployment of short-dated medicines
  • ✓Private messaging and pickup/shipping coordination
Why it matters
  • ✓Fewer abandoned scripts and therapy gaps for patients
  • ✓Lower write-offs and disposal costs for your pharmacy
  • ✓Better medication access for rural and remote patients
  • ✓Reduce contribution to $600M in community pharmacy waste
