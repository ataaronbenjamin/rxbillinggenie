The problem we solve

Billing friction is costing pharmacies time, money, and patients.

When a claim is rejected and the right intervention code isn't at hand, pharmacy staff spend 5–15 minutes on hold with insurers — for every single occurrence. Multiply that across a full day of prescriptions and the cost compounds quickly.

A patient hands over a prescription. The claim comes back rejected. Your billing binder has no answer. You're on hold with the adjudicator for 12 minutes while the queue builds. You finally get through — only to find the medication is out of stock. Rx Billing Genie and Rx MedMatch solve both sides of this problem.
