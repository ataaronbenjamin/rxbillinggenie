Pharmacy Leader of the Week: Shelley Morgan

Dr. Shelley Morgan earned her BSc in Biology & Environmental Science from the University of Western Ontario before completing her Doctor of Pharmacy at SUNY Buffalo. She went on to practice at Chedoke-McMaster Rehabilitation Hospital, supporting Stroke and Acquired Brain Injury programs, before building her career in community pharmacy.

Her daily frustrations managing insurance claims — losing upward of 15 hours a month chasing approvals — led her and pharmacy assistant Nicola Sancho-Persad to create Rx Billing Genie: a practical billing reference tool designed to help pharmacists and technicians bill accurately and efficiently.

On what drives her: "To make the lives of my fellow pharmacists and team members easier."

Her advice to pharmacy graduates: "Speak up and push beyond your comfort zone."

"The limitless possibilities, not only patient related but the industry itself — that's what excites me most about pharmacy's future."
